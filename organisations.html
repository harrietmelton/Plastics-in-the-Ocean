There are many organisations that focus on cleaning the ocean from plastics such as the ocean clean-up and 4ocean. They help by clearing floating plastics and recycling them into reusable items to sell to customers and promote this problem. This money goes back into the business to help fund more of their cleanup operation.



The things that are already being done to help stop plastic pollution are methods that do use digital technology’s such as “skimmers” from 4Ocean that are river boats that gather floating rubbish in their “arms”. The Ocean Cleanup has developed a new “Interceptor” Which forces floating rubbish onto a conveyer belt and places it in dumpsters evenly, which makes it easy to be driven to a recycling center. They also have another concept, which collects plastic in the ocean. A floating arch follows the drift of the ocean, however is slower because there is a parachute attached to it, so it collects ocean plastic, and then a boat will later take it away.
