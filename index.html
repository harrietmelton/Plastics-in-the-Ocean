Plastics in the ocean

Every year, millions of tons of plastic enter the oceans, of which the majority spills out from rivers. A portion of this plastic travels to ocean garbage patches, getting caught in a vortex of circulating currents. If no action is taken, the plastic will increasingly impact our ecosystems, health, and economies.

Recycling

What can you recycle?
does it help stop the issue of plastic pollution

This issue is something that we should be seriously thinking about because we are the people that can make change happen. From little things like recycling and putting litter in bins, we can all make a difference and reduce the amount of plastic that is entering our oceans. We are the only people that can stop this issue, so we must do as much as we can to solve it.
